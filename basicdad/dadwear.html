Basic Dad Style


Some of the more common fashion choices of the Basic Dad.

Basic dads have a "unique" sense of style. Here are a few examples of some common things that basic dads tend to wear.
This will help you to spot them in the wild.

And if you find yourself liking and wearing these same things. This may be helpful for you, to know if you are becoming a basic dad.


Common Tops-


- T-Shirt- With bands, logos, or with “funny” message on them. Most people have a novelty t-shirt or two in their closet, but the Basic Dad has ones that are just outdated enough not to be trendy or cool. Doesn't matter to this guy. He will wear them in a completely non-ironic way. Odds are, a Basic Dad probably has a Big Dog or Panama Jack shirt somewhere in his closet.

To go with that…. There's rarely a circumstance where a Basic Dad won't have his shirt tucked into their pants, even if it's a tanktop, sweater, button-up, Polo, Halloween costume, or whatever. A Basic Dad has gotta tuck.

- Oddly Buttoned Shirts- often “Hawaiian tourist” or floral print shirts
If a Basic Dad is wearing a shirt with buttons on it, he isn't going to play by your rules. He's either going to have way too many buttons done or far from enough, like Tom Selleck from Magnum PI. It's all or nothing with buttons. All torso, or no torso whatsoever.

Common Bottoms-


- Jorts or Cargo Shorts- Often with excessive pockets If there's one thing Basic Dads have in common with John Cena, it's the love of jean shorts. Which honestly doesn't make a lot of sense. Denim doesn't breathe well at all, so if it's hot enough to wear shorts, wouldn't you want maximum comfort? Basic Dads do not have time to listen to your logic and reasoning.

Shoes/Footwear


- White New Balance Sneakers- (possibly slightly tinted green from grass stains) These are a staple in every Basic Dad’s wardrobe. They're perfect for business, pleasure, or the casual 3 on 3 pick-up game.

- Sandals- (with socks) Basic Dads have to keep it classy. That's why they keep those trademark dress socks on even when wearing the most casual of shoes. It's a lethal combination.

- Tube socks- pulled all the way up Not only is it a bizarre fashion choice to wear those long, black socks with a pair of shorts, it also kind of defeats the purpose of wearing shorts when your socks cover as much leg as a pair of jeans. This is a non-issue for the Basic Dad

Accessories


- Braided Belts- Pants or Shorts are always belted. Often with this leather wicker style belt Some think belts are used to hold up your pants, but not the Basic Dad. Basic Dads will make room for a belt even if his jeans are too small. It's more about just knowing that leather is wrapped around his waist than it is for any practical reasons. If he could wear a belt with swim trunks, he would be all over it.

-Transition Lenses- Most conversations with a Basic Dad reach a point to where you're only talking about how long it took you to drive from one point to another. That's because Basic Dads love saving time. So it's only natural that dads don't have time to switch from glasses to sunglasses. Transition lenses are a dad dream come true. (Oversized or Aviator sunglasses will also work in a pinch)

- Baseball hat or Wide-Brimmed hat – Usually sporting logo of some sort. It doesn’t matter if it “goes” with the style or not, Basic Dads have to rock a hat. The bigger brimmed the better. That's why they insist on wearing hats in the style of Smalls from The Sandlot on every family outing.

- Fanny Packs- Let's face it sometimes a "normal" amount of pockets just aren't enough. Fanny packs are functional and cool. In the mind of the Basic Dad, he's looking tough while holding his stuff.

- Belt cell phone caddy or belt clip with phone attached- You know how you're constantly looking for your cell phone because you can't remember where you left it? Basic Dads have solved that problem by keeping it holstered directly to their hips at all times. It might not be pretty, but it gets the job done.

- Overly bloated wallet- A Basic Dad saves those coupons, punch-cards, and old receipts; creating quite the heavy brick in that back pocket. (Good thing hes got that belt to keep those pants held up) A Basic Dad has got to be prepared. You never know when those punch-cards and coupons might save a few cents here and there.

- Wife’s purse or A diaper bag- Though not always the case, Basic Dads can frequently be seen holding one of these in public. Most commonly seen outside of the dressing room when they go shopping with their wife.

- Fancy Key Chain Could be a hand-made gift from a child, a pinecone, or some other trinket. Or maybe a souvenir from a previous family vacation. But Basic Dads got to have a little bit of “dad bling” to show they’re a #1 dad.



Don't be judgemental of basic dad attire, even if their style is a little weird.
One day it just might be you sporting this quirky fashion.
