2. Site Purpose

My idea is simple. This site is humor site. It is intended celebrate some of the fun eccentricities of the basic dad life. Perhaps in the future this could branch out into something more substantial or commercialized. However, for now this would just be a fun fan-site to highlight the comedy of basic dads.

3. Scenario

*Who is the target audience for this site? - Young Dads, Old Dads, and Prospective Dads.
*Why would anyone want to visit this site? - For humor, advice, information on being a dad.
*Will this site provide me with useful information (aside from the humor) - Yes, additional links will be included.
